Q:  Can I cancel my reservation anytime?

A:  You may only cancel within 45 minutes of your current booking.

Q:  Is it possible to change my current booking?

A:  No, the only way to do this is to cancel the current booking and make a new one.

Q:  What is the next step after reserving?

A:  After reserving, simply present your digital ticket/s either through your mobile devices or a printed copy on the Online Resevations Line at W&Js Cinemas and pay for your physical ticket/s.
